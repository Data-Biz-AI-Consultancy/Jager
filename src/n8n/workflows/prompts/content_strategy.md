# Content Strategy by Tier 🎯

## Strategy Overview
*   **Never pitch cold** — Build awareness first (HUM), then let SING and SHOUT convert.
*   **Always-on > campaigns** — Decision makers research evenings and weekends too; consistency beats bursts.
*   **Blend both personas** — Do not silo them. Your Data Biz credibility is built on your operator track record as a data leader.

---

## Tiers & Details

### 🟢 HUM — Always-on (3–4x/week)
**Goal:** Low-effort, high-frequency posts to remain consistently visible.
*   **Data Leader Hat (Operator/Tech):**
    *   Hot takes on dbt, Snowflake, or AI tooling.
    *   Quick lessons learned from your day job at Vestiaire.
    *   Reactions to recent industry news.
*   **Data Biz Hat (Founder/Consultant):**
    *   "What I'd tell an SME owner about their data stack today."
    *   Short observations on founder pain points.
*   **Formats:** Text posts, short commentary, polls, reshares with your POV added.

### 🔵 SING — Campaign-style (2–3x/month)
**Goal:** Medium-effort, high-engagement content to drive deeper interactions.
*   **Data Leader Hat (Operator/Tech):**
    *   Career journey posts and retrospectives.
    *   Lessons learned throughout your career.
    *   "How I built X" technical breakdowns.
*   **Data Biz Hat (Founder/Consultant):**
    *   Mini case studies.
    *   Solving "accidental analyst" pain points.
    *   Repurposed Substack articles (light version).
*   **Formats:** Carousels, short videos, infographics, newsletter teasers.

### 🔴 SHOUT — Cornerstone (1–2x/quarter)
**Goal:** Big cornerstone pieces to achieve maximum reach.
*   **Data Leader Hat (Operator/Tech):**
    *   Annual state-of-data takes and predictions.
    *   Personal milestones with key strategic lessons attached.
*   **Data Biz Hat (Founder/Consultant):**
    *   Research-backed posts (e.g., "SMEs and data: what's broken").
    *   New service or consultancy launch announcements.
    *   Substack deep-dives.
*   **Formats:** Long-form posts, document posts, fully repurposed Substack articles.
